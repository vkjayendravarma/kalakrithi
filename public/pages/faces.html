Dramatics (FACES)

Picturization of daily life situations and incidents in an effective way is a challenging task. Kalarithi's drama team, Faces takes up some such social issues that require spreading of awareness regarding current crucial changes in our society. We have reached some eminent touchstones while following our passion. Every person in the cast has the ability to enact expressions of any emotion genre. Our script writers have constructed interesting plays that involved various categories of dramatics.Mimes, Nukkad Nataks and Creative Dramas are a few categories taken up by our Faces’ team. Get ready to bring out the actor in you! Faces is waiting!

judging criteria

ACTORS | WRITERS AND DIRECTORS | INSTRUMENTALIST

ACTORS
1. We need actors who can get into the skin of any character.
2. Contestants may be asked to respond to different scenario.
3. They should come prepared with one act of their own, either group or solo. Time limit : 3mins.
4. Next we will give our own scenarios and contestant's expressions, body language, acting and voice will be tested.

SCRIPT WRITER AND DIRECTORS
1. A writer should have a strong, confident writing voice and should be passionate about the same.
2. Come prepared with stage play of your own. You may have to explain how you will be directing the same.
3. You will be given on-spot content and scenario to pen down.
4. All languages are welcomed : Telugu, Hindi and English

INSTRUMENTALIST
1. People who can play instruments like dhols,drums,flute or any Non electrical classical Muscial instrument.
