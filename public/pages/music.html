Music

Music is usually the only medium that can grab the attention of any person irrespective of the state of their mind. Kalakrithi's music club is one such mesmerizing team that has skilled singers and musicians who can capture your hearts right away. Be it Indian Classical or Heavy Metal, we are always ready to perform to entrall your senses. Music club has given many such melodious performances on various occasions. Come, perform and be a part of the next marvelous performance.

judging criteria

We are looking for -

  • Vocalists
  • Instrumentalists:
    • Keyboard
    • Guitar(bass, rhythm,lead)
    • Drummers
    • Violinist
    • Flutists
    • Etc. (Any instruments)

You will be judged on -

1. Musical Theory
2. Time Signatures
3. Scale Flexibility
4. Versatility
* It is mandatory for instrumentalists to present an Original Composition
