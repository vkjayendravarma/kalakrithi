Dance

Kalakrithi's dance club comprises of a unique set of performers who bring out the rhythm of the music alive. We encourage the introduction of new styles and forms of dance and give them a chance to express their emotions in the best form. Our team 'NEMESIS' has won many prestigious competitions held by institutions like IIT Kharagpur and NIT Warangal. At the end, it is not only about becoming perfect and competitive dancers, but meeting other people with same interests and getting to know the other aspects of their talents.

judging criteria

1. The performer should be spontaneous.
2. Should be able to cop up with the team.
3. A good knowledge on what kind of style he is representing.
4. The participants can come up with a solo, duo or a group performance; its upto them. But the judgement depends upon every individual's performance, whether the whole group may get selected or only one from them.
5. Song track on which they perform shouldn't exceed 2-3 minutes.
