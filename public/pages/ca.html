Creative Arts

Art, Artist, and artistry may seem to be three distinct things but only a flawless combination of the three can make a passionate and talented artisan. It takes a lot of vision and persistence to achieve a particular standard of professionalism in any form of art. We, at Creative Arts, explore all forms of art which include portraits, abstract paintings, sketching, Quilling, Doodling, Anime, Charcoal arts, 3D-Modelling, Story writing, Column Writing, and Poetry. Our team has amateur and professional artists who have materialized many of their artistic ideas through the Kalakrithi’s platform.

judging criteria

All the Art works will be Judged basically on three things:
1. How Creative is your Art
2. How Crisp and clear/ fine is your work
3. How much time you take
Rest of the criteria is different for different art forms they present
