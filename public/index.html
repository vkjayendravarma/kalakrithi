About

Kalakrtihi is the most happening club in GITAM University. We have a variety of talented people involved in various ventures in order to showcase typical forms of Music, Art, Dance, Drama, Literature, and Anchoring. We provide a front for all the artists to showcase their passion. We believe in the ability to see and present the artistic side of ordinary daily life situations and things. We are a team running successfully since 2008. Our founders were people who chased their passion with dedication and they wanted to establish a place where people could learn new things and grow along with the changing needs and requirements of the forms of artistries. Kalakrithi is a place for the artist in you to learn and shine through the journey of pursuing their passion.
